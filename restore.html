Usage Instructions

Before You
Run

Start at the lower end of the dosing range and increase gradually over a few tanks.
Recheck needle settings after adjusting oil content -- a richer needle may be needed.
Higher oil percentage may require richening the needle slightly to compensate.
Shake bottle thoroughly before each use to ensure compounds are fully suspended.
Store in a cool, dark location away from direct sunlight and heat sources.
Not for use in 4-stroke gasoline engines. For gas 2-stroke engines use MPCZ Gas.
