Formula Development

Minimum Friction

Maximum-concentration film-forming lubricants and Molly compounds reduce boundary and hydrodynamic friction to the lowest achievable values under race conditions.

Peak Power Output

By reducing parasitic friction losses, more combustion energy is converted to crankshaft rotation. The formula has an added 30ml of 99% pure Nitromethanol boost, delivering a measurable increase in usable power.

Thermal Stability

Race-grade synthetic esters remain fully stable at the extreme temperatures generated during continuous full-throttle competition running.

Sustained Performance

Protection does not degrade across race duration. MPCZ Race maintains its performance profile from the first lap to the last, with no fade.

Usage Instructions

Before You
Run

Start at the lower end of the dosing range and increase gradually over a few tanks.
Recheck needle settings after adjusting oil content -- a richer needle may be needed.
Higher oil percentage may require richening the needle slightly to compensate.
Shake bottle thoroughly before each use to ensure compounds are fully suspended.
Store in a cool, dark location away from direct sunlight and heat sources.
Not for use in 4-stroke gasoline engines. For gas 2-stroke engines use MPCZ Gas.
