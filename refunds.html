Policy

Refunds
& Returns

Order Issues

If your MPCZ order arrives damaged, leaking, or incorrect, contact us within 7 days of delivery with your order number and a photo of the issue. We will send a replacement or issue a full refund at no cost to you.

Unopened Products

Unopened, unused bottles in their original packaging may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The customer is responsible for return shipping unless the return is due to our error.

Opened Products

Because MPCZ fuel additives are consumable chemical products, opened or used bottles cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons. If a product fails to perform as described, contact us directly — we evaluate these on a case-by-case basis and stand behind our formulas.

How to Request a Refund or Return

  • Email us with your order number, the product purchased, and the reason for your request.
  • Approved returns will receive instructions for sending the product back.
  • Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days of an approved return or claim.

Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled for a full refund as long as they have not yet shipped. Once an order has shipped, our standard return policy applies.

Damaged in Transit

MPCZ ships from Bergen, Norway. In the rare case a bottle is damaged during shipping, we will replace it free of charge — just send us a photo of the damaged packaging and product.

Contact

For any refund, return, or order issue, reach out through our contact email and we will respond within 1–2 business days.
